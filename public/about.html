About

A quiet place for ideas worth keeping.

ReidCodex is a small content and article site — a place to publish news, guides, updates, and resources. The public side is open to everyone; the admin dashboard is reserved for the site owner.

It's built static-first: clean HTML, refined typography, and a touch of JavaScript on the front end, backed by a lightweight Node.js + Express server that stores posts in a simple, portable data file.
